Ottawa Animal Rescue

• Deliverables: Web design

• Date: March, 2024

• My role: UI/UX Designer

In March 2023, during my time at Algonquin College, I was assigned a project to redesign a website called Siberians of the Heartland, which specializes in selling Siberian Huskies.

The main purpose of this assignment was to practice and improve my skills in user interface and user experience design. Through this project, I focused on creating a more engaging and user-friendly platform to enhance the browsing and purchasing experience for potential buyers.
