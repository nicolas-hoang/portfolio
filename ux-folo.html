FOLO landing pape

• Deliverables: Landing page design

• Date: November, 2019

• My role: UI/UX Designer

FOLO is a lifestyle app that encourages friends and strangers to organise hangouts. Instead of basing decisions on merchants’ discounts, FOLO reverses this process.

Users will first set the intent of their hangouts, issue an invitation, then pick a merchant to patronise. As vouchers issued by merchants no longer become a “norm”, users will experience a reduction of “voucher fatigue”. Of course, merchants are more than welcome to be generous with their vouchers. By doing so, FOLO will not alienate any marketing and promotional intent.

Merchants can also participate in in-app activities such as check-ins or social sharing to distribute exclusive vouchers. This allows merchants to strategically control their voucher distributions without compromising their availability for hangouts to take place within their premises.
