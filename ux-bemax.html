Bemax

• Deliverables: CRM Design

• Date: November, 2019

• My role: UI/UX Designer

BEMAX is an enterprise VR tool used in the construction, architecture and interior design indusDeliverablestry.

Its objective is to reduce resource wastage and keep all relevant parties updated on the project status.

Using the VR headset, professionals and homeowners can collaborate with each other in real time via voice communication. All parties get to annotate and provide feedback within the VR space, as well as check out building attributes (such as color, weight, size, material etc).

BEMAX also has a live stream function which gives non-VR users a virtual tour around the building.
