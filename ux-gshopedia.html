Gshopedia

• Deliverables: CRM Design

• Date: November, 2019

• My role: UI/UX Designer

GSHOPEDIA is a gamified voucher mobile app that allows you to engage your customers in a fun and engaging manner.

Create Mascots and build ad campaigns that target customers near your stores. With GSHOPEDIA, you don't have to chase after customers. They will come to you... simply because they are having fun!

•⁠ ⁠Geo-location draws in customers from the area to your store!
⁠• ⁠Pick and customize Mascots to represent your brand.
•⁠ ⁠Customize your campaigns by setting the location and time.
•⁠ ⁠You have the tools: Vouchers, Brochures, Videos!
