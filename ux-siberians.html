Siberians of the Heartland

• Deliverables: Web design

• Date: March, 2024

• My role: UI/UX Designer

In March 2024, during my time at Algonquin College, I was assigned a project to redesign a website called Siberians of the Heartland, which specializes in selling Siberian Huskies.

The primary objective of this assignment was to apply and practice the design theories learned in the UX Design course. This project centered on designing a more engaging and user-friendly platform to improve the browsing and purchasing experience for potential buyers.
